Yahoo for Creators is a new program giving writers a platform to share their stories with millions of Yahoo readers.

Stand out with ease

As an aggregator of content, Yahoo partners with the world's biggest publishers. Our simple tools were designed to help you craft engaging content quickly, so you can look good next to notable publications.

Make money in more ways

To support your creativity, we offer a competitive 50/50 ad revenue share from ad placements in your articles as well as e-commerce benefits like affiliate revenue share. Plus, there are no monthly fees or paywalls, so readers can always have free and easy access to your content.

Reach millions of new readers

Yahoo has over 100 million monthly U.S. visitors*, which means more eyes on you. Effortlessly share your stories with an entirely new audience and grow your following while you're at it.

Unlock actionable insights

Our intuitive analytics dashboard can help you understand reader engagement. See trends and detailed breakdowns laid out in a digestible way so it’s easy for you to know how to optimize your content.

Join a vibrant community

From fitness gurus to fashionistas, Yahoo for Creators is a home for writers with unique perspectives. Even more, it’s a community that cares about sharing ideas and expertise through high-quality content.
